## 🛠 The Ecosystem

Our hardware is built on a custom framework prioritizing reliability and network sovereignty.

### **Apex Core**
The heartbeat of the home. A universal bridge designed for dynamic Matter instantiation, allowing your Zigbee and local-protocol devices to communicate seamlessly without an external internet connection.

### **Crosswalk**
A battery-powered, door-mounted person-counting sensor. Using Time-of-Flight (ToF) technology and the ESP-NOW protocol, it provides hyper-accurate occupancy data while maintaining a minimalist footprint.

## 🔒 The Local-First Manifesto

Most "smart" homes are just "someone else's computer" in your living room. We are changing that.

* **Zero Cloud:** Your data never leaves your network. 
* **Local-Only Control:** If your internet goes out, your home keeps breathing.
* **Privacy by Design:** No cameras, no microphones—just intelligent hardware.
* **Open Documentation:** We believe in the right to repair and the right to know exactly how your hardware works.
